About us

  • Mission - preaching the gospel of Jesus Christ to Nationwide

    Nairobi West Diocese is one structure composed of several churches across the country these include:

    Gomongo Gaf Church (Located in Nairobi Dandora Luckysummer bridge) The main cathedral.

    Dandora Gaf Church (Located in Nairobi Dandora area).

    Mombasa Gaf Church (Located in Mwishomoroni).

    Ndiru Gaf Church (Located in Game Siaya).

Great Altercal fellowship motto is based in Mathew 11:28-30

It is a complete to accomplish the great commission message that "Repentance and remission of sins should be preched in his name among nation beginning at Jerusalem" Luke 24:17

The Nairobi West Diocese is committed to spreading the Gospel and serving the community through various programs and services. Established in 2002, it has grown to include several churches across Kenya. The diocese focuses on youth engagement, community service, and spiritual growth.

Our mission is to create a supportive environment where individuals can come together to worship, learn, and grow in their faith. We offer a range of services including Sunday School, youth programs, and community outreach initiatives.

Join us at one of our services or community events and become a part of our vibrant and growing community.

Our Programs

  • Sunday school

    Sunday School is one of the activities practiced by all the churches under the structure of Nairobi West Diocese. Bishop Okalo with the elders has done more on improving the Sunday school. Furthermore, raising their school fees supporting them at large.

  • Praise and Worship Team

    When Great Altercal church started, they did not engage much in praise and worship until Elder Ken Ondoro realized the importance of the Worship team. The church at large has dwelled on this and we have many youths who have done their own songs after being given a platform to worship.

  • Poultry farming for the diocese

    Poultry farming is one of the projects that Nairobi West Diocese church practices. This has created jobs for the youths and reduced drug addiction that affected the church areas.
